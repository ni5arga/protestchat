protestchat is **pre-alpha and unaudited**. Do not rely on it as your only way to stay safe. Prefer tools and habits you already trust. Only install a build you compiled yourself, or one from a source you have reason to trust.

## What it is

Encrypted messaging with **no internet, no cell tower, and no server**. Nearby phones carry messages for each other over Bluetooth. If someone walks out of a jammed area with the app open, they can carry queued messages with them.

## What you need

- Two phones (Android is the practical path today; iOS is experimental)
- Bluetooth on
- The app kept **on screen** while you need it to send, receive, or relay
- Someone to meet **in person** once to exchange contact codes
